George Good Egg Coddlers

We are listing these coddlers, tentatively, under the manufacturer name of George Good. We don't know for certain if George Good is the manufacturer, the artist, or the distributor of these coddlers.

The example of the coddler that we have seen has a cardboard tag stuck to where the backstamp would be. The cardboard tag contains the words George Good and Japan. This example is concave, with a square lifting ring.

If you know anything else about these coddlers, or if you have examples that are not shown here, please contact us.
