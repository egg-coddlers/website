Severn - Pattern SE-UNK16 (Libra)

This pattern consists of a rabbit by a pair of scales that seem to be full of eggs. There is the inscription Libra on the coddler. This coddler is larger than other Severn coddlers: 5" tall and 3½" in diameter.
