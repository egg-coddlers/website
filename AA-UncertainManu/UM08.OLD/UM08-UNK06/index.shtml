Pattern UM08-UNK06

These coddlers are double-sized cylindrical coddlers. The pattern is a flow-blue vine with flowers and leaves around the body of the coddler. A ring of floral markings is at the top and bottom of the coddler.

This coddler was for sale in the 2001 Williams-Sonoma catalogue (set of four for $24.00).

These coddlers have a backstamp, with the incriptions: WILLIAMS-SONOMA, Flow Blue (in large script), DISHWASHER AND MICROWAVE SAFE, MADE IN CHINA.
