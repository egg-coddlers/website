Unknown Manufacturer - UM08 (Sold by Williams-Sonoma)

There are several egg coddlers that are cylindrical in shape, all were sold by Williams-Sonoma at some point in time. We are not sure that all were made by the same manufacturer.

The earlier versions of these coddlers have round stickers on the bottom that look like backstamps. The most recent ones have a backstamp.
