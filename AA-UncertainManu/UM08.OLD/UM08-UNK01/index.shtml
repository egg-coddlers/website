Pattern UM08-UNK01

These coddlers are double-sized cylindrical coddlers. The glaze is a pale cobalt blue. The inside of the coddler is white. These coddlers have no backstamp sticker. The weight of this coddler (to distinguish it from UM08-UNK02) is 7.5 oz. To remove the lid of this coddler, approximately 1/3 a turn (120 ° is necessary.
