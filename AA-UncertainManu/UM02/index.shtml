Uncertain Manufacturer - UM02

These coddlers are double-sized convex with no pedestal. The pattern consists of a version of the classic Willow pattern in blue and white. These are similar to the Royal Worcester version of the Willow pattern. These coddlers have no backstamp.

    Note: --  Most metal-liddered coddlers can be damaged if the lid is screwed back on to the coddler while it or the coddler rim is wet. The two pieces of metal rust and fuse, and then part company with the porcelain when one next attempts to unscrew the lid. Usually, this can be corrected without much effort, but sometimes the rust weld is just too strong. This particular coddler seems very susceptible to this. Three of the five coddlers of this type that I've seen have had the problem. I suspect that this is because the chrome plating is especially thin and the surface area of contact between lid and coddler is especially large.
