Unknown Manufacturer - UM23 (Mary Mugg / ENESCO)

These coddlers are double-sized cylindrical coddlers.

There are several different patterns, with a common theme: fuzzy birds and flowering branches. There are also variations within a given pattern, suggesting maybe several different decals for each - or maybe a degree of hand-painting. In addition, there are other porcelain objects that we've seen with the same backstamp text as these coddlers: © 1977 MARY MUGG and LICENSEE ENESCO. In some examples, the word JAPAN also appears on the bottom.

We believe that these coddlers were sold in sets, shown below.
