Unknown Manufacturer - UM86

Single size egg coddler with a nautical theme: an anchor, a lifebelt, an inscription in French: IL ETAIT UN PETIT NAVIRE. This inscription is the name of a traditional French song.

This coddler looks and feels exactly like a Royal Worcester standard size coddler, but has no backstamp. It may well have been a special release from Royal Worcester.
