Pattern UM03-UNK03 (Two Birds)

These coddlers are double-sized convex coddlers with no pedestal and have corrugated sides. The shape is the same as coddlers made by House of Prill but they have no backstamp. The pattern consists of two coloured birds. One has blue, black, green and a belly of yellow with orange spots. The other bird's head is green and black with blue and black back and yellow belly. The birds are on a green branch with small fuchsia pink flowers and three tiny orange flowers on a top branch. This design appears on only one side of the coddler.
