Pattern UM03-UNK28 (Pair of Birds - Red & Blue and Orange & Blue)

These coddlers are double-sided convex coddlers with no pedestal. They have corrugated sides. The shape is the same as the shape of coddlers made by House of Prill, but these have no backstamp.

This pattern is a pair of Birds - one Red & Blue, and the other Orange & Blue).
