Pattern UM03-UNK06 (Fruits)

These coddlers are double-sized convex coddlers with no pedestal and have corrugated sides. The shape is the same as coddlers made by House of Prill but they have no backstamp. There is what seems to be a hand painted signature on the bottom, that may be E E Ross but it's very difficult to see.

The pattern consists of two apples, a pear, two plums, and two cherries.
