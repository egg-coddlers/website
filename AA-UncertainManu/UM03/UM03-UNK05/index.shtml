Pattern UM03-UNK05 (Plain White)

These coddlers are double-sized convex coddlers with no pedestal and have corrugated sides. The shape is the same as coddlers made by House of Prill but they have no backstamp. The pattern is a plain white glaze.

Some of these coddlers have a removable sticker marked Lillian Vernon 1981 Japan. Example 3, shown below, was found with the original box that was labelled Lillian Vernon.

Another example had a clear sticker on the bottom marked Distributed by: Distribue Par, INNOVATION, North Vancouver B. C. V7P185, and JAPAN

Yet another example had a sticker marked ROWOCO. This was probably sold by Rowoco.
