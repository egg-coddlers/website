Pattern UM03-UNK11 (Red Flowers)

These coddlers are double-sized convex coddlers with no pedestal. They have corrugated sides. The shape is the same as the shape of coddlers made by House of Prill, but these have no backstamp. This pattern is red flowers on stalks wnith a butterfly inspecting the scene. The design appears on only one side of the coddler.
