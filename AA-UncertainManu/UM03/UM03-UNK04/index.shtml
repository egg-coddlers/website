Pattern UM03-UNK04 (Rows of Tiny Flowers)

These coddlers are double-sized convex coddlers with no pedestal and have corrugated sides. The shape is the same as coddlers made by House of Prill but they have no backstamp. The pattern consists of two rows of tiny flowers, very similar to a pattern by House of Prill: HP-UNK01.
