Pattern UM03-UNK09 (Two Birds)

These coddlers are double-sized convex coddlers with no pedestal and have corrugated sides. The shape is the same as coddlers made by House of Prill but they have no backstamp. The pattern consists of two coloured birds. This design appears on only one side of the coddler.
