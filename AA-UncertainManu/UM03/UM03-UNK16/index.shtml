Pattern UM03-UNK16 (Blue Chintz)

These coddlers are double-sized convex coddlers with no pedestal and have corrugated sides. The shape is the same as coddlers made by House of Prill but they have no backstamp.

The pattern consists of a blue chintz pattern.
