Pattern UM03-UNK19 (Bunch of Blue Flowers - with a Red Ribbon)

These coddlers are double-sided convex coddlers with no pedestal. They have corrugated sides. The pattern is identical to pattern Windsor - WIN-UNK15, and the shape is the same as the shape of coddlers made by House of Prill, but these have no backstamp.

This pattern is a bunch very delicate blue flowers with a red ribbon. The design appears on only one side of the coddler.
