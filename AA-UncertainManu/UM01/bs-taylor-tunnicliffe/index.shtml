Unknown Manufacturer - UM01: Taylor Tunnicliffe Backstamp

This backstamp is embossed with TAYLOR TUNNICLIFFE & CO LTD MADE IN ENGLAND. We don't know if this is the name of the retailer/distributor or the name of the manufacturer. Two notes of interest though ... Taylor Tunnicliffe & Co. manufactured ceramics, and the name Taylor appears on Patent 49405 that appears on another backstamp. The lids on the coddlers with this backstamp are different from other UM01 examples.
