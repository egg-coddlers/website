Unknown Manufacturer - UM01: Temple & Crook Backstamp

There are four variants of the Temple & Crook backstamp that we know of: Two variants are imprinted TEMPLE & CROOK Ltd Motcomb Street S.W.1., Rd No 636774, U.S.A. Patent No 49405, the Temple & Crook logo and either MADE IN ENGLAND or BRITISH MADE. The third variant has all the information except reference to where made. The fourth variant comprises the company logo with RD NO 636774 and U.S.A.PATENT NO 49405.

Note that the registration number and patent number are the same as on the UM01: Patent 49405 backstamp.

We do not yet know whether Temple & Crook is the name of the retailer/distributor of the coddlers, or the name of the manufacturing company.
