Unknown Manufacturer - UM61 (Red and Blue Flowered Pattern)

This is a double-size coddler, with a large pedestal and a round lifting ring. The pattern consists of red and blue stylised flowers around the rim, with a red line around the base. We have only ever seen these coddlers for sale in Germany, and suspect a German Manufacturer.

The packaging says GLOBE SHOPPER, Schlemmer-Ei (Gourmet Egg), mit rostfreiem Deckel (with stainless lid).
