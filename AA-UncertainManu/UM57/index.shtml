Unknown Manufacturer - UM57 (Chintz Flowers)

This double-sized convex coddler with no pedestal is almost cylindrical in shape. It has a round lifting ring.

The pattern consists of a chintz-like pattern of blooming pink flowers around the body of the coddler.
