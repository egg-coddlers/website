UM49 are not Egg Coddlers

After careful study and consultation with other collectors, we've come to the conclusion that the objects that were assigned UM49 are, in fact, decorated Japanese tea cups, with covers. We've included some pictures in our introductory page with examples of "What an Egg Coddler is Not" (within the scope of this site).
