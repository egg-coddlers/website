Unknown Manufacturer - UM51

These coddlers were made for the mass market or for everyday use (instead of your nice collectable coddlers). They come in three colours: white, yellow, and blue. They were sold (in 2001) by The Home Marketplace, in pairs.

These coddlers are 4½ inches tall from the base to the top of the lifting ring. They are cylindrical in shape, with a recessed pedestal. There is no backstamp or other markings on the coddler. The lifting ring is rectangular and curved at the top.

The packaging indicates that these coddlers were Made in China for R.S.V.P. International, Inc. Seattle WA.
