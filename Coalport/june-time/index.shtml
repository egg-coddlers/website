Coalport - June Time

This pattern consists of a beautiful group of flowers and a butterfly. The pattern appears twice on the coddler ("front" and "back"). There is a backstamp variation that includes the name of the pattern. This is a double-sized coddler (4" tall and 2½" in diameter). The backstamp may include either the pattern name or a number (for example 38).
