Coalport - Strawberry

This pattern consists of strawberries on the plant on one side, with a stunning blue butterfly on the other. There are at least two variations of the butterfly, one blue, and one yellow (picture to be added soon). This is a double-sized coddler. There is gold trim at the base of the pedestal. This backstamp is the usual backstamp, with the number 19 below the backstamp.
