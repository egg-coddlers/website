Coalport - CO-UNK01 (Spray of Flowers)

This pattern consists of a double spray of flowers, in pink, orange, white, and red. There are three slightly different decals, applied in four places. The backstamp is marked with ROYAL CHINA, a crown, COALPORT, MADE IN ENGLAND, and EST 1750. This is a double-sized coddler. There is gold trim at the base of the pedestal. The number 9 appears below the backstamp.
