Coalport - CO-UNK08 (Blue Willow)

This is the classic Blue Willow pattern. Backstamp says ENGLAND, a crown, COALPORT, A.D. 1750, and PATENT. This is a single-sized coddler. Coalport made another Blue Willow pattern, in a double size. We are not sure what Coalport's official name for this pattern was.
