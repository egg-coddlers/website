Coalport - CO-UNK07

This pattern is in blue, with crinkled blue and white leaves - of a plant the name of which escapes me. The backstamp is marked with ENGLAND, a crown, COALPORT, A.D. 1750, and PATENT. This is a single-sized coddler.

Note: We know of a version of this coddler that also has a gold band in the design. We do not yet have a picture.
