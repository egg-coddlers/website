Coalport - Ludlow

This pattern consists of a bunch of flowers, including one pink rose, one white flower, several smaller red flowers, and a yellow narcissus. The pattern is repeated on the other side of the coddler. There is gold trim at the base of the pedestal. This backstamp is the usual backstamp, with the number 47 below the backstamp.

This is a double-size coddler.
