Wedgwood - Charnwood

The front decal of this pattern consists of a pink flower with stem, two green leaves, and two small purple flowers with a brown butterfly on the front. The decal used on the back varies. "Decal A" has a red and blue flower with crossed stems. "Decal B" consists of pink and purple flowers similar to the the front. "Decal C" is very similar to "Decal B" except that a stem extends from the large flower, and a purple butterfly is alighting on the stem.
