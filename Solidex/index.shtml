Solidex Egg Coddlers

 We know of the existance of at least one egg coddler by this manufacturer because it recently appeared for sale at auction on eBay.

The seller of this coddler had it with the original box and instructions for its use English and French. All the lettering on the outside of the box is in English. The coddler was distributed under licence by an English firm called Murell Ltd for the French manufacturers. The seller believes that this coddler dates from the 1950's.
