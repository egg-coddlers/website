CPIAG Coddlers

We're assuming that this jar with a screw-threaded lid is a coddler. It may have been intended for another use. These coddlers are convex with a small pedestal and no lifting ring. These were made in Czechoslovakia.

The pattern is a flower garland with red roses around the centre, with a frieze around the top of the coddler.

The backstamp consists of the words CPIAG, and Czechoslovakia ... surrounding a design.

These coddlers are very similar in style and pattern to those from Unknown: UM06, Unknown: UM07, Unknown: UM28, and Unknown: UM54,
