Roy Kirkham - New English Flora Pattern

This pattern consists of a spray of flowers, dominated by vetch and bluebells. This is the second pattern that Roy Kirkham issued with the same name. The "New" part of the name is of our invention. The pattern appears twice, on opposite faces of the coddler. (Click on either image to view in more detail). The copyright date of this pattern is 2002.
