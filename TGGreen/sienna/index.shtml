T.G. Green - Sienna Pattern

These coddlers are convex, with a pedestal, and have a a flat lifting ring ... that is, a flat strip of metal, approximately ¼ inches wide, curved into a circular loop, and bonded to the lid.

The pattern is a cream-coloured coddler with two red-brown strips near the rim. We have verified examples of this coddler with three of the T.G. Green backstamps. Some are also imprinted with the pattern name in black as part of the backstamp.
