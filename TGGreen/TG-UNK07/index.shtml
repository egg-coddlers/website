T.G. Green - Pattern TG-UNK07

These coddlers are convex, with a pedestal, and have a a flat lifting ring. This coddler design is unlike many of the other T.G. Green convex coddlers, in that the "hips" of the coddler are much more pronounced.

The coddler is decorated with a spray of pink flowers as the main theme with many smaller pink flowers all around. The pattern surrounds the coddler and seems to extend to the base as well.
