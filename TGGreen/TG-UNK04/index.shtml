T.G. Green - Pattern TG-UNK04

These coddlers are made of pottery, including the lid. The body of the coddler is convex, without a pedestal. The lids have a "primitive" screw thread, and a "flattened-oval" lifting ring.

This pattern consists of a light tan finish with the words I'm a good egg in black script.

TG-UNK04 Pattern

The dimensions of these coddlers are as follows:

  • Height without lid: 31/8 inches
  • Height with lid: 4¼ inches
  • Lid height: 15/8 inches
  • Base diameter: 17/8 inches
  • Diameter at the rim (inside): 113/16 inches
  • Diameter at the rim (inside): 27/16 inches
