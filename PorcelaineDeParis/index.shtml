Porcelaine de Paris Egg Coddlers

Porcelaine de Paris manufactured two styles of egg coddler: concave, and convex with a pedestal.


Concave Coddlers

These coddlers are double-sized concave coddlers.

The backstamps are marked PORCELAINE DE PARIS, FRANCE, a pair of crossed arrows, and either DEPUIS 1773 or FONDEE EN 1773, the pattern name, and in some cases, a number.

North American Bird Coddlers

These coddlers are double-sized convex coddlers, with a pedestal, and are decorated with various North American bird species. We've been told that there are six different coddlers in this set, but have only found names for three at this time.

The backstamps are marked PORCELAINE DE PARIS, FRANCE, a pair of crossed arrows, without the pattern name.
