Royal Worcester - Black Cats Pattern

This pattern consists of a black cat, shown from the front, back, and side. The background is yellow and blue with some red floral things, and a red floral border between the three decals.

This pattern is believed to have been first used by Royal Worcester on egg coddlers in 2001.
