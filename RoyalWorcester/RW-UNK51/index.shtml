Royal Worcester - RW-UNK51 (Purple/Grey Flower Sprays)

This pattern is in a purple/grey (or perhaps blue/grey) ink that looks like it was pen-drawn. The pattern consists of loose bunches of flower sprays on one decal and a single spray of the flowers on the reverse.
