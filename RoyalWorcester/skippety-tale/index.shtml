Royal Worcester - A Skippety Tale Pattern

This pattern consists of a happy group of bouncing animals with vegetables, fruit, and flowers. Depending on the size you are looking at, you'll see mice on an apple, hedgehogs, a rabbit with a carrot, butterflies, a squirrel with acorns (on the Maxime), etc. This is an incredibly cute egg coddler.
