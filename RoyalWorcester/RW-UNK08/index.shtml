Royal Worcester - RW-UNK08 (Flower on Purple Stem)

There are several decals shown on different examples. The decals all consist of pink, red, or yellow flowers, some growing from bamboo, some growing from the ground. We're not certain whether Royal Worcester had a single pattern in mind, or if there were random decals available that the coddler decorators could select as they saw fit. For now, we'll simply document the examples that we know of and see what we learn.

If you have a coddler in your collection with one or more of these decals that is different from the examples shown, we'd love to hear from you. Please do contact us if you have new information about this pattern.
