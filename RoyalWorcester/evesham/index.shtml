Royal Worcester - Evesham Pattern

There are many variations of the pattern. Most of them have a plum (sometimes described as a peach) and other fruit, including blackberries, cherries, currants (red and black) and sometimes a leaf. In some cases, decals were applied that did not include the plum at all. Here are the permutations of the types that we've seen to date. There are sure to be more.
