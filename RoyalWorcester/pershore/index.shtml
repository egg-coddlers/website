Royal Worcester - Pershore Pattern

This pattern consists of combinations of a single pink rose, rose hips, gooseberry, and on the doubles, either blue flowers or blue plums.

    Note: Although this design was confirmed as "Pershore" by the Royal Worcester Museum, I have also see a saucer with either this or a very similar pattern that was called "Ashford". -- BB
