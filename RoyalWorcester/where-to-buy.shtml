Where to Buy Recent RW Egg Coddlers

Royal Worcester (RW) still produces and sells their egg coddlers as a retail item. Bruce says that they also have, in the past, and probably will again, make up a few coddlers of whatever design is handy when they have space in their kilns. Perhaps these are sold at the RW shop, in Worcester, England.

I have not yet found a web page for Royal Worcester (RW) online. It is only a matter of time before they have one. We will maintain a list here of retail establishments, online or "brick and mortar," where we've seen or heard of RW coddlers for sale. Your local gift shop is probably a good place to look for them too ... there might be one gathering dust on a shelf. Listing here is not an endorsement of these vendors ... just a point of reference.

  • Always Porcelain, Ltd.

  • China Etc

  • The English China Shop

  • First Ireland Giftware

  • giftCOLLECTOR.COM

  • Harrods - An E-mail correspondant has told us that she has seen RW coddlers for sale at Harrod's in London (UK).

  • Small Island Trader - look in the Gift Shop section

If you know of places where coddlers are available for retail sale, please let us know and we'll update the page.
