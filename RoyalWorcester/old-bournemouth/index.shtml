Royal Worcester - (Old) Bournemouth Pattern

This pattern consists of small blue, red, and yellow flowers, with a brown "branch" around the middle of the coddler.

    There is another RW design called Bournemouth. I have seen a saucer in this design with Bournemouth in the backstamp. -- BB

Editorial Note: Lest we create any more confusion in RW historical nomenclature, we shall refer to this pattern as (Old) Bournemouth and the other pattern as Bournemouth.
