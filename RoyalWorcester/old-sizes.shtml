Sizes of Royal Worcester Coddlers Produced

Royal Worcester (RW) produced three size of coddler.

The Standard Size was designed to prepare one egg. It measures 3½ inches from the base to the top of the lifting ring. With the lid off, the coddler body stands 2½ inches tall. This size can also be used to warm up an individual serving of baby food.

The King Size was designed to prepare two eggs. It measures 4 inches from the base to the top of the lifting ring. With the lid off, the coddler body stands 3¼ inches tall. It is approximately 2½ inches in diameter.

The Maxime Size was designed to prepare a small individual meal, such as a small stew. It measures 5¼ inches from the base to the top of the lifting ring. With the lid off, the coddler body stands 35/8 inches tall. It is approximately 3¾ inches in diameter. RW no longer produces this size of coddler.

Some details of specific patterns will only appear on the King Size or Maxime Size coddlers. The following patterns are the only ones that we know were produced in a Maxime size: A Skippety Tale, Astley, Bournemouth, Country Kitchen, Evesham, Palmyra, Pershore, Peter Pan, Rhapsody, and Strawberry Fair.
