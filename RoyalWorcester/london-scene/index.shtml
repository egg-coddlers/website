Royal Worcester - London Scene Pattern

This pattern consists of a scene of London, wrapped around the body of the coddler. The pattern surrounds the body of the coddler. London appears three times at the bottom of the pattern.

This pattern is believed to have been first used by Royal Worcester on egg coddlers in 2001.
