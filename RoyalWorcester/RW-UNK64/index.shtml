Royal Worcester - RW-UNK64 (Stinton Pheasant)

This is an old Royal Worcester coddler. The painting of a Ring-necked Pheasant is signed "Stinton" We can't quite make-out the initial - and several members of the Stinton painting dynasty worked for Royal Worcester. The backstamp is Type 4C.
