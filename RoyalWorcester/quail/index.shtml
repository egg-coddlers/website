Royal Worcester - Quail Pattern

This pattern shows a walking quail, with or without a signature beneath the bird. The reverse side of the coddler is plain white.

We are not sure that the official Royal Worcester name for this pattern was quail.

If you have example of any of the "bird" patterns ( Quail, Bobwhite, Canada Goose, Mallard, or Pheasant ), with a clear signature (or clear portions of the signature), please contact us ... we'd like to try to decipher the signatures.
