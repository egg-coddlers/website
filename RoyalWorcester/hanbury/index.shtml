Royal Worcester - Hanbury Pattern

This pattern consists of a blue motif, similar to, but in a deeper blue, and smaller, than Rhapsody.

There are two variations of the king size coddler, one that has the same large design backand front, and the otherthat has a butterfly on the reverse (the same as the standard size). There are also two variations of the design on the single.
