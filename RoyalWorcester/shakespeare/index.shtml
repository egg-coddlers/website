Royal Worcester - Shakespeare Pattern

This pattern consists of a scene of Stratford upon Avon, wrapped around the body of the coddler, with the face of Shakespeare superimposed, and Stratford Upon Avon appears once at the bottom of the pattern.

This pattern is believed to have been first used by Royal Worcester on egg coddlers in 2001.
