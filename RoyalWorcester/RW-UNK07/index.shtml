Royal Worcester - RW-UNK07 (World War II Plane in Flight)

This pattern shows a World War II plane in flight. One of our contacts has shown these images to people at Royal Worcester. They believe that the image may have been done by hand by someone. It isn't an official Royal Worcester decal that was ever used by the factory. It could have been done by someone in the factory, or after it left the factory. The coddler was probably a RW-UNK02 (plain white) that was later decorated with the plane.
