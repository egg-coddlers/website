Royal Worcester - Peter Pan Pattern

This pattern consists of three different images from the story Peter Pan. Each size has a different pattern.

The single coddlers show pictures of Native Americans and the text The redskins disappear as they have come -- like shadows.

The double coddlers show pictures of an Native American princess and the text The Beautiful Princess and Tiger Lily.

The maxime coddlers show pictures of fairies and the text Every time a child says - I don't believe in Fairies -- Another -- Fairy somewhere falls down Dead.
