Royal Worcester - Pattern RW-UNK23 (Teal Blue and Purple Flowers)

This pattern consists of teal, blue, and purple flowers around the coddler belly, on a green vine, with a few other flowers. The pattern includes a vertical blue ribbon with a mustard yellow dot in the centre.
