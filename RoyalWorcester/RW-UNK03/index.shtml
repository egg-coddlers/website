Royal Worcester - RW-UNK03 (Purple on White)

RW-UNK03 -- Purple on White

This design is purple on white, purple around the top third, with a gold "necklace" underneath. This coddler has a silver top and thread, hallmarked Birmingham, 1912. The backstamp is in red, and has registration number 561564.
