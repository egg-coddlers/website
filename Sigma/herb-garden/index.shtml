Sigma - Herb Garden Pattern

This design is found on a convex coddler with a pedestal.

The coddler is 4 inches from the base to the top of the lifting ring.

This pattern consists of green bunches of herbs and their names. The herbs are: tarragon, parsley, rosemary, wild watercress, and sage.

There are at least two backstamp varieties.

The backstamp variety referred to as simple consists of the words HERB GARDEN. The backstamp variety referred to as complete consists of the name, HERB GARDEN, and TASTE SETTERS EAST and JAPAN in a box.
