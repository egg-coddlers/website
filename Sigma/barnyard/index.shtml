Sigma - Barnyard Pattern

This design is found on a convex coddler with a pedestal. The pattern consists of wire mesh, a pair of chicken feet, and feathers.

The backstamp is inscribed TASTE SETTER COLLECTION. There are two variations of the lid, one chrome, and one gold coloured. We've seen both types with a square lifting ring, but there are probably others.
