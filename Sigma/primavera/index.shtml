Sigma - Primavera Pattern

This design is found on a concave coddler with no pedestal. The pattern consists of flowers, including a large red poppy. There are at least two backstamp varieties, and at least two lifting ring varieties.

The backstamp variety referred to as simple consists of the words TASTE SETTERS: JAPAN. The backstamp variety referred to as complete consists of the name, PRIMAVERA, and TASTE SETTERS: MADE IN JAPAN in a box, with a couple of flowers.
