Aynsley - Cottage Garden Pattern

This pattern consists of a garden scene, with flowers, plants, and a butterfly. On the back is a vine with leaves and a flower. There are two variations of the flower on the back of this pattern. One is an orange-yellow flower, the other is a blue and yellow three-leaved flower. There are also two variations of the ring on the lid, with a round cross-section, one with a flat cross-section.
