Egg Coddlers with K.Z.E. or KAZ Backstamps

These are double sized coddlers. The ceramics are all the same raised "paneled" design, but the backstamps are marked either KZE or KAZ. We do not yet know which company made them, or where, but we suspect either Germany, or, more unlikely, Switzerland.


Patterns

KZE-UNK01

Four panels, three of chickens, and one with the coat of arms of Neuchatel, Switzerland.




KZE-UNK02

Four panels of chickens - the same as KZE-UNK01, but without the coat or arms, and with a different backstamp.




KZE-UNK03

Decorated with Cherubs. The backstamp is unknown.




KZE-UNK04

Decorated with a blue freize.



KZE-UNK05

Plain White



KZE-UNK06

Blue Flowers
