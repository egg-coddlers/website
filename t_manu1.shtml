Manufacturers of Egg Coddlers

This is a catalog of companies that have been known to manufacture egg coddlers at some point in time. On some coddlers, the manufacturer can be easily identified by the backstamp. In other cases, the packaging material, inserts, or stickers tells us something about the manufacturer. We are always looking to expand our knowledge of coddlers ... If you have something that isn't mentioned here, please do contact us.

Notes

    1 - The editors of this web site don't know who is the manufacturer of these coddlers. We've grouped them together based on those that we believe were made by the same manufacturer.

    2 - These unknown manufacturer codes are entirely our own creation/fabrication. We use these codes to avoid confusing coddlers that might be from different manufacturers. Of course, we don't know. We will assign numbers sequentially, and once assigned, a number will not be reused, even if we eventually discover the actual manufacturer.
