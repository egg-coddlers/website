Chadwick Egg Coddlers

These are convex porcelain cups with white Bakelite lids that were sold as egg cookers. The lid sits on top of the body, without any threading or strap to hold it down. These coddlers were sold in pairs with a dipping stand with which to lower them into boiling water and lift them out. They come in both clear and opaque white versions.
