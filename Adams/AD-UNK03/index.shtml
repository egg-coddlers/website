Adams - Pattern AD-UNK03 (ABC's/123's)

This pattern consists of the letters of the alphabet, in upper and lower case, and the numbers from 1 to 20, around the body of the coddler. Both red and blue versions exist.

The examples we've seen to date all have a Type 2 backstamp.

Both the single size (approx. 3½" tall) and the double size (approx. 4" tall) are known.
