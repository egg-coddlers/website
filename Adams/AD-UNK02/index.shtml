Adams - Pattern AD-UNK02 (Rooster/Porridge)

This pattern consists of a rooster on one side of the coddler and the words Save your breath to cool your porridge. on the other side. We've seen this coddler only with an Adams Type 1 backstamp.

Both the single size (approx. 3½" tall) and the double size (approx. 4" tall) are known.
