Egg Coddlers by Sands

Egg coddlers that are double-sized with concave body style and a square lifting ring. We don't know whether these were manufactured by Sands or manufactured by a third party and distributed by Sands.

We know of two series of patterns by Sands: Holly Hobbie and Tiny Talk. The pattern identifiers are our own -- we don't know what the manufacturer's pattern name is.

We are interested in hearing from collectors who have these, or similar egg coddlers in their collection. Please contact us if you do.


Holly Hobbie Patterns

These egg coddlers are decorated with images of Holly Hobbie and a homily. The backstamps we've seen include the text: Designer's Collection, HOLLY HOBBIE® by Sands, GENUINE PORCELAIN, © W W A, Inc. USA, a date in Roman numerals and MADE IN JAPAN.

Tiny Talk Patterns

These egg coddlers are decorated with images of Tiny Talk small animals (mouse, cat, turtle) and a homily.
