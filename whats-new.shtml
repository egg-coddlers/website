What's New at Egg-Coddlers.COM?

This page will no longer be updated. Changes to the site will be announced on the new page: News.

This page is a register of changes made to the Egg-Coddlers.COM web site, and news from the editors.

We continue to count on egg coddler collectors to inform us when you find a new pattern, or discover information that is different from what is already on the site. We have an immense backlog of material to enter, so please be patient if you have sent updates to us and the page hasn't changed yet. If you are concerned that your communication has been missed or misplaced, please contact us again.


April 13, 2019

    Reports of the death of this web site are premature! The editors have been enjoying life, which means that we have not been updating the content of the web site. However, we continue to collect new information and corrections to existing information, and it will be added to the web site as time allows.

    We are also working (slowly) on a revamp of the site ... moving the content from the early 1990's CMS (manually editing HTML text files, one at a time). It is too early to point to a preview of the new web site, although this page will probably be the first to move.

  • So the Contact page seems to have been broken by the Google ReCAPTCHA changes. We put in a temporary fix, which is to give our direct email address
