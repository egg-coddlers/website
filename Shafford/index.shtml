Shafford Coddlers

Egg coddlers that have a backstamp identifying them with Shafford do not indicate where the coddler was made. Other pieces of porcelain made by Shafford have Japan in their backstamps.

Shapes

    Shafford coddlers are known in two shapes: concave, without a pedestal, and convex with a pedestal.
