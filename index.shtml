Welcome to... Egg-Coddlers.COM

This web site (Egg-Coddlers.COM) is dedicated to the study of egg coddlers.

An egg coddler is a porcelain or pottery cup with a lid that was designed to prepare food ... in particular, an egg dish that is called coddled eggs.

This site intends to be an information resource and a forum for collectors and users of egg coddlers.

Note: The data shown on this web site should not be assumed to be exhaustive or even complete. We have large amounts of uncatalogued data that has yet to be added to this site. We need your help to make this site useful. If you know of manufacturers, patterns, recipes, examples, or any other information ... or if you have images of egg coddlers that you would like to share on this site, please contact us.
