Pattern WS-UNK02

These coddlers are double-sized cylindrical coddlers. The glaze is a deep cobalt blue. The inside of the coddler is a cream colour. These coddlers have a backstamp sticker that says Made in China, Williams-Sonoma, and Grande Cuisine, with the reversed "GC" logo in the centre. The weight of this coddler (to distinguish it from WS-UNK01) is 7 oz. To remove the lid of this coddler, approximately 2/3 of a turn (240 o) is necessary.

This coddler was for sale in the 1997 Williams-Sonoma catalogue (set of four for $20.00).
