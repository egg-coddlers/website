Pattern WS-UNK04

These coddlers are double-sized cylindrical coddlers. The pattern is a flow-blue flower (rose?, tulip?) with a blue ring at the top and bottom of the coddler.

This coddler was for sale in the 2000 Williams-Sonoma catalogue (set of four for $24.00).
