Pattern WS-UNK03

These coddlers are double-sized cylindrical coddlers. The pattern is a flow-blue cluster of leaves with a blue ring at the top and bottom of the coddler. These coddlers have a backstamp sticker that says Made in China, Williams-Sonoma, and Grande Cuisine, with the reversed "GC" logo in the centre.

This coddler was for sale in the 1998/1999 Williams-Sonoma catalogue.
