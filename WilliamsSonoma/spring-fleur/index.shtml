Spring Fleur Pattern

These coddlers are double-sized cylindrical coddlers. The pattern is a flow-blue, flowers and butterflies around the body of the coddler with a solid stripe at the top and bottom. According to the catalog, these are hand-painted.

These coddlers were for sale in the 2005 Williams-Sonoma catalogue (Item # 73-6668594, Set of four for $26.00).

These coddlers have a backstamp, with the incriptions: WILLIAMS-SONOMA, Made in China, Dishwasher Safe.
