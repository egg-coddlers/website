Harker Egg Coddlers

We don't know if these were intended to be used as egg coddlers. These are barely convex ... really cylindrical with a slightly curved in base. The lid sits on top of the body ... it's made of aluminum with a knob handle. The backstamp says HOTOVEN, HARKER, THE OLDEST POTTERY IN AMERICA. These objects might have been intended as condiment jars. Some were sold in sets of four with a wire rack.
